Our History

Founded during the COVID-19 pandemic, Express Errands emerged from the need to address the challenges of obtaining essential goods and services. The owner recognized the difficulties faced by individuals and businesses alike and set out to create a reliable delivery solution for the community.

Since then, we have evolved to meet the demands of our customers, focusing on efficiency and customer satisfaction, while establishing ourselves as a trusted name in the delivery industry.

FAQS

Get answers to common questions about our services

We deliver to all areas across Sierra Leone, including urban and rural regions. Our riders are equipped to handle deliveries in diverse locations.
Delivery times vary depending on the destination, but we strive to deliver packages within 24 hours for local deliveries and within 2-3 days for long-distance deliveries.
You can track your package through our website by entering your tracking number. We also send updates via email and SMS.
Our pricing varies depending on the package size, weight, and delivery location. You can contact us or use our pricing calculator on the website for detailed information.
We handle all kinds of packages, including documents, parcels, and larger items. However, for very large or heavy packages, please contact us in advance to ensure proper handling.
Scheduling a delivery is easy! You can either use our online form on the website or call our customer service team to arrange the pickup and delivery time.
